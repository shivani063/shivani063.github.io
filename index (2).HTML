Computer Operator and Programming Assistant (COPA) :-
Computer Operator and Programming Assistant ek aisa course hai jo aap 10th ke baad kar sakte hai Is course me aapko Computer se judi bahut see jankari sikhne ko milegi Jisse aap ek Safal computer operator ban sakte hai or is course me aapko basic computer programming bhi parichay karaya jayega. Yah course sirf 1 year ka hota hai. to agar aap koi diploma like DCA, ADCA aadi karne ke baare me vichar kar rahe hai to usse behtar ITI ka yah course hoga.
Scope : Is trade me ITI karne ke baad aap kisi bhi department me as a computer operator ke taur par job prapt kar sakte hai.

D T P Operator :-
DTP operator yani Desk Top Publishing Operator is course me aapko DTP work jaise ki Photo editing, Document, Video, banner aadi banana sikhaya jata hai yah course bhi 1 year ka hota hai or iske liye minimum qualification bhi 10th paas hona jaruri hai.
Scope : DTP operator me ITI karne ke baad aapko print media jaise news paper, Printing press, TV aadi me job mil sakti hai. ya aap khud ka DTP work bhi start kar sakte hai.

Stenography Shorthand :-

Scope : Is field me ITI karne ke baad aap Govt ke kai department jaise court, Police, Mantralay, Railway aadi me job kar sakte hai.
